The New Way to Protect Mushroom Farms

FreshSense uses smart smell-detection technology to catch mold and bacteria before it spreads.

Why FreshSense?

  • Prevents crop loss – detects problems early
  • Saves money – avoids costly harvest damage
  • Reduces stress – monitors 24/7
  • Improves mushroom quality – healthier, better-looking crops
  • Works for small and large farms

How It Works

  1. Smart Odor Sensor: Placed in grow rooms, detects unusual odors automatically.
  2. Mobile App: Alerts farmers immediately with clear messages and color indicators.
